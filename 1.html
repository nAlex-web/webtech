There must have been something unique or, at least, different about me as a boy, because I recall it would sometimes amuse my brother and his friends to throw beer cans at me. Why? It was my father who encouraged in me the notion that I and I alone am responsible for my own life, for what I do and don't do, for my opinions and beliefs, and it's proved to be a great source of strength.
